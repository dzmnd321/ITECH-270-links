My Favorite Things

K-pop

One of my favorite things is K-pop, I really like the music that some of the artists create. My favorite band is Stray Kids because they have a more alternative style to their music, along with interesting music videos and an overall nice dynamic between the members. The culture of K-pop is more like a community within the fanbases of many of the groups. I also feel like it's a good way to bring people of differing backgrounds together because there are fans that have made friends from all over the world due to their interest in certain bands.

Cooking

Another of my favorite things would have to be cooking. I really enjoy making new kinds of dishes for my family, and recreating dishes I've seen on the internet. I really like to make a lot of Hispanic and Korean food. I make two dishes in particular pretty frequently because they're always asked for, which would be Taquitos and Kimchi Jigae. Taquitos are basically small rolled up tacos that I deep fry, and Kimchi Jigae is a Korean stew made with kimchi and pork belly and various seasonings, usually served with rice.

Tiktok

I like Tiktok. It seems pretty self-explanitory but other than the obvious funny videos I see, it helps give me ideas on what to cook and stay moderately updated on things going on in the world. I do admit that I spend quite a bit of time scrolling, but I find that it is a good resource for me when it comes to certrain things. I use it for tips on cleaning, pet care, clothing and cooking, as well as recommendations for music, new anime shows, and new small brands and companies.
